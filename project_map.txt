TITLE : Alien invasion


Player controls a rocket ship that appears at the bottom of the screen
Player can move ship right and left using arrow keys and shoot bullets using spacebar.

Beginning : A fleet of alien ships appears and move across the screen.

Every new wave of alien ships moves faster than the previoius wave.

OBJECTIVE : PLayer moves ship and shoots alien ships.
If an alien ship hits the player's ship or reaches the bottom of the screen, game ends.
If the player loses 3  ships, - GAME OVER
